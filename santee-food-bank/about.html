About Us

The Food Bank provides emergency food assistance to low and middle income residence of Santee. We provide a food pack to residents one time per month. We are an all volunteer organization with no paid salaries. Our highly committed staff disperses food from our location: every Wednesday and Friday and the first & third Saturday from 9-11 AM.

The Food Bank was established by some of the churches of Santee. Santee Food Bank is under auspices of The Santee Ministerial Council, which was established in 1983.
